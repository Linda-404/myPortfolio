Short story

Born in stockholm but moved to a small town called charleswood at an early age. Grew up in a “Slim Susie” community ( movie reference) and rocked through grunge and indie pop phases with glam.

Moved to norway in 2008 with my sister, living on cigarettes and fishsticks, working part time at a kindergarten until I landed a fulltime job as a veterinary assistant in 2009. There on out, I have been working in the veterinary industry until 2020. Working with animals in the medical field was really interesting and rewarding in many ways. I really loved it but it came to a point I felt that my growth stopped and I needed to levitate my creative personality.

So I took the scary step out and started to pursue the dream of becoming a webdesigner. I chose to study front-end development as I wanted to learn the language of the web and all the possibilities to be creative.

Web design to me:

A good web design should be to create a “feeling” and not a “thought” about the design, that portrays the content in a desirable fashion and gives visual meaning. And that's what I think is so exciting about it, to go from an idea, collect all the pieces and then assemble them. To experiment, try out different designs and keep up with the forever changing trends.

My Goals:

I want to build and create things that are meaningful.

Master the art of designing and code semantics to the point.
